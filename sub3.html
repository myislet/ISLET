Frequently asked questions

  • How to use a Suite Night Upgrade ?

    Booking with Suite Night Upgrades:

    The use of a Suite Night Upgrade cannot be added to an existing reservation nor directly at the hotel upon arrival.
    Suite Night Upgrades should be redeemed when processing a new reservation only.

  • What measures have been taken with regards to members following the Covid-19 pandemic ?

    What measures have been taken with regards to members following the Covid-19 pandemic ?

    Given that the current health crisis is affecting travel and travel plans,the ISLET loyalty programme wants to support members
    dealing with this unprecedented situation and is implementing measures for their benefit :)

  • Why have I lost my point? How can I get it back ?

    Why have I lost my point? How can I get it back ?

    Awarding and keeping different statuses is dependent on your activity in the ISLET loyalty programme.
    The loyalty programme offers 5 statuses awarded according to the number of Status Nights spent or the number of Status points
    earned over a calendar year (from January to December). Participation in the loyalty programme starts with Classic status.
